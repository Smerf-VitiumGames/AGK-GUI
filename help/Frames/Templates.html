          Templates are collection of gadgets assigned to a window. They take some time to build but once set up, they are worth the trouble.

To build a template first create a function for your template.

MyTemplateFunction()

Endfunction

Inside your function you will create a Window Main=MakeWindow()

 Next, build buttons labels or other gadgets and add them as children to the window.

It is important to give your elements a name so templates can search themselves for targets. 

 

 

MyTemplateFunction()

MainWindow=MakeWindow()

Mybutton=MakeButton()

//ID,name

SetButtonName(MyButton,”MyButtonName”)

addChild(MakeButton,MainWindow)

Endfunction

 

Gadgets run faster than templates, but templates allow for fast easy replication and essentially your own custom gadgets with less work.
